Plant Profile

Marigold

A vibrant, easy-growing flowering plant widely used in festivals, decoration, and natural pest control.

About this plant

Marigolds are cheerful, bright flowering plants that bloom almost year-round in warm climates. They are famous for their golden-yellow and orange blossoms and are often used in garlands, puja rituals, and garden decoration.

These hardy plants are also natural pest repellents, making them a popular choice for companion planting in vegetable gardens.
