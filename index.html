    Seasonal care at a glance

    How plants behave through Indian summer, monsoon, and winter.

    Summer

    • Move delicate plants from harsh sunlight.
    • Water early morning or evening.

    Monsoon

    • Reduce watering.
    • Prevent fungus in poorly drained pots.

    Winter

    • Growth slows down.
    • Keep away from cold windows at night.

    Care basics for nearly every plant

    Four pillars that keep most indoor plants healthy.

    Light

    Bright, indirect light suits most plants.

    Water

    Underwater slightly rather than overwater.

    Soil & Pot

    Use drainage holes to prevent root rot.

    Air

    Good airflow prevents pests and fungus.
