Plant Profile

Rose

A timeless flowering plant admired worldwide for its beauty, fragrance, and huge variety of colors.

About this plant

Roses are one of the most iconic flowering plants, known for their stunning blossoms and pleasant fragrance. They thrive in sunny environments and bloom best when provided with proper pruning and care.

With countless varieties, roses can grow as shrubs, climbers, or even miniature desk-sized plants. They are widely used in gardens, bouquets, perfumes, and skincare.
