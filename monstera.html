About this plant

Monstera Deliciosa is a fast-growing tropical plant known for its big, split leaves. It climbs in nature, using trees for support, and can be trained on a moss pole or stake indoors.

This plant loves bright, indirect light and slightly moist soil. If you give it enough space and support, it will reward you with dramatic new leaves that unfurl with fresh holes and splits.

Seasonal behavior

  • Monsoon: Reduce watering slightly to avoid soggy soil.
  • Winter: Growth slows down; keep away from cold drafts.
