Plant Profile

Monstera Deliciosa

A bold indoor tropical plant known for its iconic split leaves and jungle-like growth.

About the Monstera

The Monstera Deliciosa is one of the most popular tropical houseplants worldwide. Its dramatic split leaves create a lush vibe that instantly transforms any corner into a mini-jungle. Native to the rainforests of Mexico and Panama, this plant grows by climbing trees and spreading wide.

Monsteras love warm, humid environments and filtered sunshine — similar to their natural forest habitat. With the right care, they grow fast and reward you with new leaves that open up with fresh holes and splits.

Care Requirements

Light

Bright, indirect light. Avoid harsh sunlight.

Water

Water weekly. Allow the top soil to dry slightly.

Soil & Pot

Well-draining mix: cocopeat + perlite + compost.

Pet Safety

Not pet-safe. Keep away from cats & dogs.

Seasonal Behavior

  • Summer: Grows fastest; loves humidity.
  • Monsoon: Water less to avoid root rot.
  • Winter: Growth slows; reduce watering.

Quick Info

  • Scientific Name: Monstera deliciosa
  • Type: Indoor tropical
  • Care Level: Medium
  • Ideal Room: Living room, study
  • Growth Speed: Fast-growing
  • Mature Height: 2–7 feet indoors
