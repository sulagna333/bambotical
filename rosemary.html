Rosemary

A woody Mediterranean herb that brings flavour and fragrance to both your balcony and your kitchen.

About this plant

Rosemary is an evergreen herb with needle-like leaves and a strong aroma. It‚Äôs often used in cooking for potatoes, breads, pasta, and roasted veggies.

It prefers full sun and well-draining soil. In pots, it does best when you avoid overwatering and give it plenty of airflow around the foliage.

Use a gritty, well-draining mix (add sand or perlite).

Harvest

Snip the soft tips for cooking; avoid removing too much at once.

Seasonal behavior

  • Summer: Enjoys the sun; water slightly more if windy and hot.
  • Monsoon: Make sure the pot drains well; too much rain can cause root rot.
  • Winter: Growth slows; keep in a bright spot and water less often.
