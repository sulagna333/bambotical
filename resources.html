Plant Resources & Tips

Simple guides for watering, soil, light levels, seasonal care, pests, and more.

Watering Basics

Most plant deaths come from overwatering. Follow these rules:

  • Check soil before watering.
  • Let soil dry for succulents.
  • Bigger pots stay wet longer.
  • Water until it drains out.
  • Use room-temperature water.

Perfect Soil Mixes

Different plants prefer different soils.

Indoor Foliage Plants

  • 40% potting soil
  • 30% cocopeat
  • 20% compost
  • 10% perlite

Succulents & Cactus

  • 50% coarse sand
  • 30% cocopeat
  • 20% perlite

Seasonal Care Guide

Summer

  • Water morning/evening.
  • Give shade to soft plants.

Monsoon

  • Reduce watering.
  • Watch for fungus.

Winter

  • Use warm water.
  • Keep tropical plants warm.
